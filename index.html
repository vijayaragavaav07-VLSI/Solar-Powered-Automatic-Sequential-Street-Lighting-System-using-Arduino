⚡ INTRODUCTION

The Solar Powered Automatic Street Lighting System leverages renewable solar energy combined with intelligent LDR-based sensing to autonomously control street LEDs. Built on an Arduino Uno R3, this system eliminates manual intervention — lights turn ON at dusk and OFF at dawn automatically, reducing energy waste and operational costs. The system continuously monitors ambient light levels and responds in real-time, making it ideal for smart city infrastructure and rural off-grid deployments.

⚙️ WORKING PRINCIPLE
☀️
DAYTIME
Solar panel generates power → charges battery. LDR resistance drops (bright light) → Arduino reads low analog value on A0 → Pins 5,6,7 set LOW → LEDs OFF.
🌙
NIGHTTIME
LDR resistance rises (darkness) → Arduino reads high analog value on A0 → Threshold check passes → Pins 5,6,7 set HIGH → LEDs glow brightly.
🔋
ENERGY STORAGE
Battery stores surplus solar energy during daylight and powers Arduino + LEDs throughout the night cycle continuously.
🔄
AUTONOMY
The closed-loop feedback ensures fully automatic, maintenance-free operation 24/7 without any human input whatsoever.
